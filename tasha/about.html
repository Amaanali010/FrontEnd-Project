OUR JOURNEY

From humble beginnings to industry leader

1995

Foundation

TASHA was founded in Milan, Italy by master craftsman Antonio Rosetti with a vision to create premium kitchenware that combines traditional craftsmanship with modern design.

2002

International Expansion

Opened our first international showroom in New York City, marking the beginning of our global presence. Launched our flagship cookware line to critical acclaim.

2010

Innovation Center

Established our state-of-the-art Research & Development center in Frankfurt, focusing on sustainable materials and smart kitchen technology integration.

2018

Sustainability Initiative

Launched our "Green Kitchen" initiative, committing to 100% sustainable packaging and energy-efficient manufacturing processes across all product lines.

2023

Smart Kitchen Revolution

Introduced our AI-powered smart appliance series, connecting traditional kitchen excellence with cutting-edge technology for the modern home.
