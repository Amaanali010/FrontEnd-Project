WALNUT

Walnuts are premium dry fruits with a rich, earthy taste, best known for their brain-shaped appearance and omega-3 fatty acids.

Key Nutritional Highlights

  • Excellent source of omega-3 fats
  • Supports brain & heart health
  • Rich in antioxidants
  • Improves memory & focus
  • Helps reduce inflammation

Health Benefits

Walnuts are loaded with omega-3 fatty acids that help improve brain function, support heart health, and reduce inflammation. Regular consumption promotes better memory, balanced cholesterol levels, and overall wellness.
