CASHEWS

Cashews are creamy, mildly sweet dry fruits known for their rich taste, smooth texture, and impressive nutritional value.

Key Nutritional Highlights

  • Rich source of plant-based protein
  • High in healthy fats
  • Supports heart & bone health
  • Good source of minerals like magnesium & zinc
  • Boosts energy and immunity

Health Benefits

Cashews are packed with essential nutrients that support overall health. Regular consumption helps improve heart health, strengthens bones, supports brain function, and enhances skin and hair health. Their healthy fats and minerals make them an excellent addition to a balanced diet.
