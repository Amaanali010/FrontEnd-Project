Mabroom dates are an elongated and elegant date variety originally from Saudi Arabia and known for their rich, sweet flavor and firm yet tender texture. They have a slightly chewier bite compared to Medjool, with subtle notes of honey and caramel. Mabroom dates strike a nice balance between sweetness and texture — making them a favorite for gifts, healthy snacking, traditional festivities, and Ramadan iftar/suhoor. Their elongated shape and appealing dark reddish‑brown color give them a premium look, and they’re packed with nutrients like natural sugars, fiber, and minerals.
