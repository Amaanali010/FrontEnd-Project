Rooftop Infinity Pool

An architectural marvel suspended above the Riviera — 42 metres of heated infinity pool with unbroken sea views, private cabanas, and a full poolside menu.

  • Open 7AM – 10PM year-round
  • Heated to 28°C / 82°F
  • Private cabana hire available
  • Dedicated towel & cocktail service
  • Children's pool adjacent

The Aurélia Spa

A 3,000 sqm sanctuary of wellness featuring nine bespoke treatment rooms, a traditional hammam, hydrotherapy circuits, and an organic skincare laboratory.

  • 9 private treatment suites
  • Hammam & steam rooms
  • Floatation therapy pods
  • Organic product range
  • Couples' rituals available

Performance Fitness

A 800 sqm state-of-the-art facility equipped with Technogym machines, a dedicated free-weight zone, and a mirrored yoga/pilates studio with harbour views.

  • Open 24 hours daily
  • Personal trainers on request
  • Daily yoga at sunrise
  • Pilates & barre classes
  • Complimentary for all guests
