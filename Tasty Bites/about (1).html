Our story

Passion · Tradition · aste

from a small kitchen to your heart

Tasty Bites was born in 2010 in a tiny family kitchen with just three tables and one dream – to serve soulful food made with love. Our founder, chef Arjun Mehta, believed that food is more than ingredients; it’s an emotion. Drawing inspiration from his grandmother’s recipes and travels across India, he started crafting dishes that soon became local legends.

Today, we are a multi‑cuisine restaurant chain with four bustling branches, but our heart remains the same: we still marinate our paneer by hand, slow‑cook our gravies, and welcome every guest like family. Every dish tells a story – of aromatic spices, fresh produce, and the warmth of genuine hospitality.
