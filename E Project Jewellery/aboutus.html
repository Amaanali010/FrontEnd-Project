Our Story

A Legacy of Craftsmanship and Excellence

The Beginning

Founded in 1985, Pascal Jewellery has been at the forefront of luxury jewelry design for over four decades. Our journey began with a simple yet profound vision: to create timeless pieces that celebrate life's most precious moments. What started as a small family-owned workshop has blossomed into one of the most trusted names in the jewelry industry.

Our founder, Master Craftsman Robert Pascal, brought with him over 30 years of experience from the finest jewelry houses in Europe. His passion for perfection and unwavering commitment to quality laid the foundation for what Pascal Jewellery represents today – a harmonious blend of traditional craftsmanship and contemporary design.

Our Collections

We specialize in curating collections from the world's most prestigious brands including Tiffany & Co., Cartier, and Bulgari, while also offering our own exclusive designs. Our expertise spans across three distinct categories, ensuring that luxury and beauty are accessible to everyone:

  • Original Collection: Featuring natural precious stones with GIA certification and crafted in 18K-24K gold or platinum. Each piece represents the pinnacle of luxury and authenticity.
  • Semi-Precious Collection: Showcasing beautiful gemstones like sapphires, emeralds, and tourmalines set in premium metals. These pieces offer exceptional value without compromising on elegance.
  • Artificial Collection: Expertly crafted pieces using high-quality materials that capture the essence of luxury at accessible prices. Perfect for everyday elegance.

Our Commitment

At Pascal Jewellery, we believe that every piece of jewelry tells a story. Our team of expert gemologists, master craftsmen, and designers work tirelessly to ensure that each creation meets the highest standards of quality, authenticity, and artistic excellence. We are committed to:

  • Using only ethically sourced materials from certified suppliers
  • Maintaining strict quality control throughout the creation process
  • Providing transparent certification for all original pieces
  • Offering lifetime maintenance and care services
  • Supporting sustainable and responsible jewelry practices

Our Core Values

The Principles That Guide Us

Authenticity

Every original piece comes with certification from recognized gemological institutes including GIA, IGI, and AGS. We guarantee the authenticity and quality of every stone and metal used in our creations.

Quality

Rigorous quality control ensures perfection in every detail. From the selection of raw materials to the final polish, each step is executed with precision and care by our master craftsmen.

Craftsmanship

We work with master artisans and jewelers worldwide who bring decades of experience and unparalleled skill to every piece. Their dedication to the art of jewelry making is evident in every detail.

Customer Service

Dedicated support throughout your jewelry journey. Our team is committed to helping you find the perfect piece and providing lifetime care services to keep your treasures sparkling.

Sustainability

Ethically sourced materials and responsible practices. We are committed to environmental stewardship and fair labor practices throughout our supply chain.

Innovation

Blending traditional techniques with modern technology to create designs that are both timeless and contemporary. We continuously evolve to meet the changing tastes of our discerning clientele.
