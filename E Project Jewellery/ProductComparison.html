Original - Premium Quality

Best For: Special occasions, engagements, investments, and heirloom pieces.

Benefits: Natural stones with exceptional brilliance, lifetime warranty, GIA certification, and excellent resale value. Crafted from 18K gold for lasting durability.

Investment Value: Appreciates over time and can be passed down through generations.

Semi-Precious - Best Value

Best For: Everyday luxury, special gifts, and quality jewelry at accessible prices.

Benefits: Lab-created or natural semi-precious stones with excellent clarity, 2-year warranty, quality assurance, and 14K gold plating for durability.

Value Proposition: Outstanding balance of quality, beauty, and affordability for daily wear.

Artificial - Fashion Forward

Best For: Fashion jewelry, casual wear, trend experimentation, and budget-conscious buyers.

Benefits: Stylish designs, multiple color options, lightweight comfort, and affordable pricing. Perfect for matching with various outfits.

Style Flexibility: Experiment with different looks without significant investment.

Understanding Quality Tiers

Original Jewelry

  • Natural precious stones (diamonds, rubies, emeralds)
  • Solid gold (18K-24K) or platinum
  • International certification (GIA, IGI)
  • Lifetime warranty & free maintenance
  • Investment-grade quality
  • Excellent resale value

Semi-Precious

  • Lab-created or natural semi-precious stones
  • 14K gold plated or sterling silver
  • Quality assurance certificates
  • 2-year warranty
  • Excellent durability for daily wear
  • Great value for money

Artificial Jewelry

  • Cubic zirconia or glass stones
  • Brass, stainless steel base
  • Quality assurance
  • 6-month warranty
  • Budget-friendly pricing
  • Fashion-forward designs
