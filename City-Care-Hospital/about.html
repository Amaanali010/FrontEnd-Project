Who We Are

A Legacy of Healing & Compassion

Founded in 1999, MediCare Hospital has grown from a modest community clinic into one of the region's most trusted and comprehensive healthcare institutions. Over the past 25 years, we have served over 500,000 patients, building a reputation for clinical excellence, cutting-edge research, and genuine compassion.

Our hospital spans a modern 12-floor facility housing 40+ specialized departments, over 500 beds, and a team of 120+ board-certified physicians, 400+ nurses, and 600+ healthcare professionals working in unison to deliver the highest standard of care.
